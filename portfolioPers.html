KOZMOZ

KOZMOZ is a spaceship fighter video game in which up to four players each take control of a ship, inspired by the common archetypes of those from the game Cosmoteer, and face off against each other in a free for all deathmatch to decide who is the greatest fighter. The game was created with GDevelop for a game jam in our Interactive Game Design class.

Celestial Unified Fleet

The Celestial Unified Fleet (CUF) is a bridge simulator community that revolves around weekly scenarios run in Empty Epsilon. Participants take on various roles in the piloting of a starship, these roles being helmsman, weapons officer, engineer, science officer, relay officer, and commanding officer. I have participated in approximately 4 sessions and have assimilated into the roles of relay and science officer as well as engineer./p>

Cosmoteer

Cosmoteer is a game in which players build starships with a grid-based construction system and fight against others. Competitive elimination matches are one-on-one or two-on-two duels with ships having a maximum budget of 1.5 million credits. I have developed a collection of four untested competitive ships, including Railsaw, Heracles, Evil Deluxe, and Final Fortress, as well as four paintcards.
