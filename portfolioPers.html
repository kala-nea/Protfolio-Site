BIT·CRUSH

Under BIT·CRUSH, I make music that combines themes and motifs of power metal with the instuments and style of chiptune. My first album, Awakened, is currently in development, and will include 7 songs in total.

Power Study

My second project is Power Study, a classification system for superpowers that encompasses all possible power types. Inspired by the sayings of Rand's Razor and the lack of a definitive class system in popular culture that adequately covers any and every possible unique power one could eveer come up with, Power Study aims to gather all of superhero media into one intuitive system that allows for complete creative freedom without compromising any inclusivity.
