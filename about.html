Hobbies

Music

Both at work and at home, I listen to a lot of music. My playlist has exceeded three full days of music with over 2300 songs, and I know most of the songs I listen to by heart. I primarily listen to power metal, Christian rock, and electronic music, and some of my favorite artists and bands are Decyfer Down, Blind Guardian, and Aviators.

Decyfer Down

Decyfer Down is a Christian rock band based in Morehead City, North Carolina. They have 6 studio albums and 1 single. The band started as an acoustic band, but switched to a heavier rock sound after Caleb Oliver joined on percussion and later bass guitar.

Blind Guardian

Blind Guardian is a German power metal band formed in Krefeld, West Germany. Their style fuses gothic- and fantasy-tinged European power metal with the velocity and technical precision of speed metal. Operating since 1984, they have 15 albums and 9 singles and are refered to as one of power metal's Big Four bands along with Helloween, Sabaton, and DragonForce.

Aviators

Aviators is a variety musician based in Fort Myers, Florida. He primarily makes music inspired by various games and shows that he enjoys such as My Little Pony and Elden Ring, and also has a series of concept albums based around the fictional regime of the Bleeding Sun. He currently has around 21 albums and 19 singles.

Games + Rec

In my free time, I like to enage in mental challenges and help others in their endeavors. Currently, I do this by playing board and table-top games such as BattleTech, and participating in STEM-related events such as FIRST Robotics Competition.

BattleTech

BattleTech is a table-top wargame in which players pilot 'Mechs (short for BattleMech) against each other, and the last player standing wins. Games are split into rounds, with rounds split into three phases in which players roll for initiative, move their 'Mechs, and fire their weapons.

FIRST Robotics Competition

FIRST Robotics Competition, or FRC for short, is a global high school sport in which teams build a robot to complete tasks given in the form of a game, then compete in weekly events. Teams play on alliances with two other teams, which can change throughout the event. At the end of each event, the top eight teams chose alliance partners to compete for a chance to go to the World Championship.
