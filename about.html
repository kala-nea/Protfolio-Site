I primarily listen to power metal, Christian rock, and swing music, and some of my favorite artists and bands are Theocracy, Gloryhammer, and Aviators.

Theocracy

Theocracy is a Christian power metal band based in Athens, Georgia. They have 4 studio albums and 5 singles, with their lead singer, Matt Smith, having three other singles with his charity project Project Aegis.

Gloryhammer

Gloryhammer is a symphonic power metal band based in Scotland. Their claim to fame is the world they have built around their music, a fictional story of prophecy and revenge between the line of Prince Angus McFife and the evil wizard Zargothrax over control of the land of Fife. They currently have 3 albums, 3 singles, and another album and single on the way.

Aviators

Aviators is a variety musician based in Fort Myers, Florida. He primarily makes music inspired by various games and shows that he enjoys such as My Little Pony and Elden Ring, and also has a series of concept albums based around the fictional regime of the Bleeding Sun. He currently has around 20 albums and 17 singles.

Games + Rec

In my free time, I like to enage in mental challenges and help others in their endeavors. Currently, I do this by playing board and table-top games such as BattleTech, and participating in STEM-related events such as FIRST Robotics Competition.

BattleTech

BattleTech is a table-top wargame in which players pilot 'Mechs (short for BattleMech) against each other, and the last player standing wins. Games are split into rounds, with rounds split into three phases in which players roll for initiative, move their 'Mechs, and fire their weapons.

FIRST Robotics Competition

FIRST Robotics Competition, or FRC for short, is a global high school sport in which teams build a robot to complete tasks given in the form of a game, then compete in weekly events. Teams play on alliances with two other teams, which can change throughout the event. At the end of each event, the top eight teams chose alliance partners to compete for a chance to go to the World Championship.
