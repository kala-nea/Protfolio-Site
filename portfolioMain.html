Web Projects

Here I have assembled four of the web-related projects I have worked on. The first is CSS Zen Garden, a playground for experimenting with pure CSS. The second is Yellow Journalism, a presentation for a social issue that I had to outsource to others, letting them create the site based off a wireframe prototype made by me. The third is a Budget Calculator, an easy automated way of calculating taxes due with just the input of a salary. The fourth is Scalawag Seas, a web game based off a physical board game.
